What is Neurodiversity?

Neurodiversity is the concept that neurological differences are to be recognized and respected as any other human variation. These differences can include those labeled with Dyspraxia, Dyslexia, Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder, Dyscalculia, Autism Spectrum, Tourette Syndrome, and others.
